Privacy Policy for Quiz Quest Adventure
Last Updated: April 17, 2026
This Privacy Policy describes how Quiz Quest Adventure ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application.
1. Information We Collect
We strive to collect only the minimum amount of data necessary to provide a great gaming experience.
A. Information You Provide
•
Leaderboard Name: If you choose to participate in the global leaderboard, we may collect a nickname or username that you provide. This name will be visible to other players.
B. Information Collected Automatically
•
Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including the hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (like Advertising ID).
•
Usage Data: We track game progress, such as scores, levels completed, and time spent in the game, to provide features and improve the app.
•
AdMob Data: Our app uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your Advertising ID and usage patterns to provide personalized ads.
•
Location Data: If you grant permission, we may use location services to improve ad relevance or provide local features.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data to:
•
Maintain and display the Leaderboard.
•
Operate and improve the game mechanics.
•
Provide haptic feedback and interactive features.
•
Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob.
•
Analyze usage trends to fix bugs and improve performance.
3. Third-Party Services
We use professional third-party services that have their own privacy policies:
•
Google Play Services: Provides the foundation for Android apps and game features.
•
Google AdMob: Delivers advertisements. Google's Privacy Policy
4. Children's Privacy
Quiz Quest Adventure is a general audience app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information (such as a leaderboard name), we will delete it immediately.
5. Device Permissions
To function correctly, the app requests:
•
Internet Access: Required for downloading quiz data, updating leaderboards, and displaying ads.
•
Vibration: Used to provide haptic feedback during gameplay for a better user experience.
•
Location: Used to provide location-based services if permitted.
6. Data Retention and Security
We retain your information (like leaderboard scores) only as long as necessary to provide the game services. We take reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or loss.
7. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, update, or delete the personal information we hold about you (such as your leaderboard entry). Please contact us to exercise these rights.
8. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
